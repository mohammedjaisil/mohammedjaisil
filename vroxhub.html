Portfolio Details

VroxHub

As the lead designer for VroxHub, I was responsible for creating a modern, visually engaging platform that reflects the brand’s focus on cinematic videography and photography. My goal was to craft an intuitive user experience that highlights stunning visuals while maintaining smooth navigation across all devices.

I designed the layout, color palette, and typography to complement the artistic nature of the content, ensuring that every element worked together to create an immersive viewing experience. The responsive design adapts seamlessly to desktop and mobile, offering users a polished and consistent look.

Through careful attention to detail, I incorporated a clean yet dynamic style, allowing the site’s visuals to stand out without distraction. By combining modern design trends with usability best practices, I helped VroxHub establish a strong online presence that resonates with its creative audience.
