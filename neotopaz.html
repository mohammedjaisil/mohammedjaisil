Portfolio Details

Neo Topaz

As the lead designer for Savory Table Restaurant, my vision was to create an elegant, appetizing online presence that reflects the restaurant’s passion for gourmet dining. The platform needed to balance rich visuals of dishes with clear, intuitive navigation for an exceptional user experience.

I crafted a refined color palette inspired by the restaurant’s cuisine, pairing it with sophisticated typography to convey a premium feel. The design highlights high-resolution imagery of chef-crafted meals, ensuring that each dish is showcased with mouthwatering detail.

The responsive design adapts seamlessly across devices, allowing customers to browse menus, make reservations, and discover special events effortlessly. Through a combination of aesthetic appeal and practical usability, Savory Table’s website mirrors the dining experience—refined, memorable, and full of flavor.
